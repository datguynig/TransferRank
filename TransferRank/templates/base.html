About TransferRank

TransferRank analyzes football transfer rumours using a sophisticated scoring system that considers source credibility, club fit, transfer value, and momentum.

Scoring Methodology

Our algorithm combines tier-based source reliability with club needs analysis, market value assessments, and rumour momentum tracking to provide transparent rankings.

How We Score
  • Credibility (40%): Source reliability and corroboration
  • Club Fit (30%): Position needs and playing style
  • Value (20%): Transfer fee vs. estimated market value
  • Momentum (10%): Recent activity and multiple sources
How to Filter
  • Search: Player names, clubs
  • League: Filter by competition
  • Position: Filter by player position
  • Fee Range: Filter by transfer value
  • Source Type: Filter by journalist, club, etc.
